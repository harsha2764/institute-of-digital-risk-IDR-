# Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) – Brand & Homepage

This repository contains the **brand identity design and responsive homepage implementation** for the **Institute of Digital Risk (IDR)** — an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on **digital risk, cyber risk, and AI governance**.

The project demonstrates the creation of a **modern brand identity** and a **fully responsive single-page website** built using **semantic HTML5, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript**.

---

# Project Objectives

The goal of this project is to design and implement:

* A **minimalist, professional logo** for the Institute of Digital Risk
* A **responsive homepage** that clearly communicates IDR’s mission and services
* A **clean, accessible web layout** without the use of CSS frameworks

The design emphasizes **clarity, modern technology aesthetics, and professional credibility** suitable for an academic and industry-facing institute.

---

# Logo Design

## Concept

The logo is built around a **cube-inspired geometric icon** representing:

* **Structure** – organized frameworks for risk management
* **Risk layers** – different levels of digital and technology risk
* **Resilience** – stability and protection within digital systems

The cube form also reflects **technical systems architecture and modular frameworks**, which are central to digital risk and governance.

---

## Color Palette

| Color      | Purpose                                                 |
| ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Orange** | Innovation, transformation, forward-thinking technology |
| **Black**  | Authority, professionalism, trust                       |
| **White**  | Clarity, simplicity, modern design                      |

This palette provides strong **visual contrast and readability**, making the logo suitable for both digital and print use.

## About IDR

This section explains the purpose and positioning of the institute.

IDR is an **industry-led training and deployment institute** focused on preparing professionals for roles in **digital risk, cyber security, and technology governance** in regulated environments.

The institute connects **academic insight through UK university partnerships** with **real-world industry implementation projects**, ensuring graduates are prepared for modern risk challenges.

---

## Service Pillars

The IDR model is built on three core pillars.

### Academy

Professional training programs and bootcamps for students and professionals entering digital risk careers.

### Innovation & Incubation

Development of new research, risk models, and **AI governance frameworks** to address emerging technological risks.

### Advisory Services

Support for organizations implementing risk and security frameworks including:

* NIST Cybersecurity Framework
* ISO 27001
* NIS2 Compliance

---

## Community

IDR supports a professional community that includes:

* University students
* Early-career professionals
* Industry practitioners

Particularly in sectors such as:

* Financial services
* Technology
* Critical infrastructure
* Highly regulated industries

The institute creates a **pipeline from training to industry deployment**.

# Technical Implementation

## Technologies Used

* **HTML5 (Semantic structure)**
* **CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid layout)**
* **Vanilla JavaScript**
* **Google Fonts**

No frameworks (such as Bootstrap or Tailwind) were used.

---

## Key Features

### Semantic HTML Structure

The page uses modern semantic elements:

```
<header>
<nav>
<main>
<section>
<footer>
```

This improves **accessibility, SEO, and maintainability**.

---

### Responsive Design

The layout adapts to different screen sizes using:

* CSS Flexbox
* CSS Grid
* Media Queries

The site is optimized for:

* Desktop
* Tablet
* Mobile devices

---

### Sticky Navigation

The navigation bar remains fixed at the top of the page and includes:

* Smooth scrolling
* Hover states
* Clear section navigation

---

### Accessibility Considerations

* High contrast color palette
* Readable typography
* Mobile-friendly layout
* Large interactive buttons
